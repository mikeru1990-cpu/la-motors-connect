PRIVACY INFORMATION

Clear about customer information.

This page explains, in straightforward language, how information submitted through L.A Motors Connect may be used.

This is a practical website privacy notice for the current enquiry-stage system. It should be reviewed as the platform grows into customer accounts, a shared database or automated messaging.

Information customers may provide

Booking, quote and MOT-interest forms may collect details such as name, mobile number, vehicle registration, vehicle information, preferred dates and notes about the requested work.

Why the information is used

Information is used to review enquiries, contact customers, discuss requested work, prepare quotes, arrange appointments and manage interest in future services such as MOT testing.

Form processing

The current live enquiry forms are processed through Netlify Forms. Customers should avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive information in free-text notes.

Retention and access

Customer information should only be kept for as long as reasonably needed for enquiries, bookings, business records and legitimate follow-up. Access should be limited to people who need it for L.A Motors operations.

Customer requests

Customers can contact L.A Motors if they have a question about information they submitted or want to request an appropriate correction or deletion.
