CUSTOMER HELP

Questions? Start here.

Quick answers about bookings, prices and common L.A Motors services.

How do I request a booking?

Open the Book Online page, choose the service and send your details. Liam can review the enquiry and contact you to confirm availability.

Is an online enquiry a confirmed appointment?

No. The form sends a booking request. The appointment is confirmed after L.A Motors reviews the job and availability.

What car key work is available?

Customers can enquire about blade keys, remote keys, spare keys, lost keys and programming. The exact option depends on the vehicle.

How much are car keys?

Current guide prices shown on the site start from £69 for blade keys and £99 for remote keys. Final price depends on the vehicle and key type.

What air-con services are available?

The site currently shows guide prices from £50 for R134a and from £80 for R1234yf. Final cost is confirmed for the vehicle and work required.

Can I request diagnostics?

Yes. Use the booking or quote form and describe the warning light, symptoms or fault as clearly as possible.

Does L.A Motors do servicing and repairs?

Yes, customers can send servicing and repair enquiries. Pricing depends on the vehicle, parts and exact work required.

Are MOTs available now?

MOT services are planned for the future. The MOT interest page lets customers register vehicle details and interest before launch.
