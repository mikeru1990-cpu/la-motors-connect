How to use the live booking system

This page explains what happens when a customer sends an enquiry and what to check inside Netlify.

1

Customer books

Customer opens the website, taps Book Online and sends the enquiry form.

2

Netlify captures it

The enquiry is stored in the Netlify Forms area for the live site.

3

Liam confirms

Liam reviews the enquiry, calls/messages the customer, and confirms the job.

Where to find enquiries

Netlify form submissions

  1. Open Netlify.
  2. Open the L.A Motors site.
  3. Go to Forms.
  4. Open la-motors-booking.
  5. Review the customer enquiry details.
Important: The dashboard on the homepage is still a demo dashboard. The real customer enquiries are the ones submitted through the booking page and captured by Netlify Forms.
